About Us

Cruise is management system that gives students the power to manage their school in a wholesome approach. It ensures that the students balances between academics,social life ,mental health,financials and any part of life that a student may be struggling with.It ensures that students "CRUISE" through their school life easily with our AI features that offer specialized services to fit very students diverse need.School life can be overwhelming but with our system,students can manage their time effectively and make the most out of their time and resources available.

Our products are well tailored to fit the needs of the studentss.Our products and service include :
  • Chatbot:An AI-driven chatbot to provide instant support and answer common questions related to academic pressure, time management, and mental health.
  • Personalized Recommendations:AI algorithms to suggest resources based on user input regarding their specific challenges.
  • Scheduling Assistant:An AI tool to help students manage their time effectively by creating study schedules and reminders.
  • Routine Analysis:The AI will analyze students' class schedules and eating habits to create personalized schedules and provide relevant advice.
  • Grade Tracking:The AI will track students' grades and offer suggestions on areas for improvement.
