About Us

Cruise is a management system that gives students the power to manage their school in a wholesome approach. It ensures that the students balance between academics, social life, mental health, financials, and any part of life that a student may be struggling with. It ensures that students "CRUISE" through their school life easily with our AI features that offer specialized services to fit every student's diverse needs. School life can be overwhelming, but with our system, students can manage their time effectively and make the most out of their time and resources available.

Our products are well tailored to fit the needs of the students. Our products and services include:
  • Chatbot: An AI-driven chatbot to provide instant support and answer common questions related to academic pressure, time management, and mental health.
  • Personalized Recommendations: AI algorithms to suggest resources based on user input regarding their specific challenges.
  • Scheduling Assistant: An AI tool to help students manage their time effectively by creating study schedules and reminders.
  • Routine Analysis: The AI will analyze students' class schedules and eating habits to create personalized schedules and provide relevant advice.
  • Grade Tracking: The AI will track students' grades and offer suggestions on areas for improvement.

Our website allows the students to create User Accounts to track their progress, access personalized resources, and participate in the community forum.This allows us to analyze the student's perfomance and give pesonalized recommendations.It also opens the student to a new world where the stress of being a student is eased.The platform also allows the students to interact and talk about daily challenges ,this gives the students a way of venting out without judgement and fear. We believe the platform will avail resources to students and easen the burden that comes with being one.
